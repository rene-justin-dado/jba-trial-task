What's this section about?




Owned media is media you own (the clue’s in the name!). It’s media that you have complete control over. These channels are great for sustaining longer-term relationships with customers. Examples include: company websites, customer databases, social media channels (Instagram, Facebook, Snapchat, Twitter, Pinterest, LinkedIn) and retail stores (window displays, point of sale displays and staff uniforms).
